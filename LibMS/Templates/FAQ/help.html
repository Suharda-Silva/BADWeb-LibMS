FAQ

- The particular person must visit the physical library in order to get registered. Address of the location has mentioned in the "infor" page.

- Any person who is in our organization has access to the library.

- Mainly we have books in English language. Apart from that we have books with languages such as Sinhala, Tamil, Spanish, German, French, Russian, Latin and Chinese etc.

- The user can borrow 3 books at a time.

- After selecting the book according to the preference, the user can click on the reserve button.

- The reservation of a book lasts for a maximum of 3 days. The administation staff has the authority to remove the reservation after 3 days.

- He/She must visit the pysical library to collect the books.

- The user must return the book within 14 days (2 weeks) from the borrowing date. Fines will be imposed for being late.

- Please check on the contact details mentioned in "infor".

- We do not accept monetory donations. For more information please check "Donate".
